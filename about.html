About Blog

Tetsu's Blog was originally created in 2020 in an attempt to reduce boredom amidst the COVID-19 pandemic. It was first hosted on Blogger, a blogging platform by Google, then was moved to Blog FC2. However, due to the limitations of these 2 blogging platforms, I decided to use Jekyll to manage my blog, and use GitHub Pages to host it.
